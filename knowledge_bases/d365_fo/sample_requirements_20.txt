Acme Corp – D365 F&O Discovery Notes
Wave 1 Finance & Supply Chain — Reduced Scope
Date: March 2026

---

AP-1: Automated Invoice Matching
Invoices need validation against purchase orders and goods receipts with configurable tolerance thresholds. Mismatches flag for clerk review before payment.

AP-2: Invoice Extraction & Ingestion
PDF/scanned documents need automatic header and line extraction linked to purchase orders and routed to approval workflow.

AP-3: Payment Batch Proposal
System proposes payment batches based on due dates and early payment discounts, filtered by vendor/currency/entity. Controller sign-off required.

AP-4: Vendor Hold Mechanism
Formal hold covering payment, invoice, and all-transaction types. Held vendors blocked from new POs, posting, and payments. Audit trail required.

---

AR-1: Credit Limit Enforcement
Enforce credit limits at order entry and invoicing. Block or escalate transactions exceeding limits to credit manager.

AR-2: Automated Collections
Auto dunning with tiered letter sequences triggered by aging thresholds. Configurable per customer classification.

AR-3: Payment Matching
Automatic customer payment matching by amount and remittance reference. Unmatched receipts to suspense account.

---

GL-1: Month-end Close Task Management
Structured checklist with task owners, due dates, dependencies, and real-time status visibility.

GL-2: Financial Reporting
User-defined report layouts (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow) with live GL data and drill-through capability.

GL-3: Budget Encumbrance Tracking
Track PO/PR encumbrances against approved budgets at dimension level. Block over-budget transactions.

GL-4: Bank Reconciliation
Auto-match bank statements (MT940, SEPA, BAI2) to GL cash entries by amount, date, and reference.

---

INV-1: Purchase Order Approval Routing
POs above threshold route through approval hierarchies by amount/category with mobile notifications and 48-hour escalation.

INV-2: Multi-vendor Bidding
Vendor portal for bid submission with comparison ranking (price, lead time, score). Accepted bid converts to PO.

INV-3: Batch Traceability
Capture and enforce batch attributes (expiry, origin, quality) from receipt through picking.

---

PM-1: Timesheet & Project Costing
Staff submit timesheets against project tasks; approved hours auto-post to cost journals.

PM-2: Milestone-based Billing
Auto-create invoice proposals at milestone completion, routed for approval before client delivery.
